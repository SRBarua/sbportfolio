Who am I

I am a Web Developer and Designer living in Calgary, Alberta, Canada. I am a passionate UI/UX designer with real-time project experience of creating Responsive websites. I always focus on designing beautiful and functional user experiences. I am a fast learner and trained in all stages of development cycle and writing clean, and efficient code. I have completed my Web Development Diploma from InnoTech College, Calgary Alberta. I also completed Manual testing and Test Automation certificate programs in Software Quality Assurance (QA). In all these programs, I have acquired industry based hands-on project experiences.
